SAVVARI COMMUNITY & SAFETY GUIDELINES
Effective Date: [INSERT DATE]
IMPORTANT NOTICE
These Community & Safety Guidelines (“Guidelines”) establish the standards of behavior expected from all Users of the Savvari Platform.
These Guidelines are incorporated into and form part of the Savvari Terms of Service.
By using the Platform, you agree to comply with these Guidelines at all times.
Violations may result in warnings, ride restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, removal of content, cancellation of rides, reporting to law enforcement authorities, or other actions deemed appropriate by Savvari.
Savvari reserves the right to investigate and take action against conduct that threatens the safety, integrity, trust, reputation, operations, or legal compliance of the Platform.

1. PURPOSE OF THESE GUIDELINES
Savvari is designed to facilitate respectful, safe, and responsible non-commercial carpooling between independent Users.
These Guidelines are intended to:
	•	promote safety and trust;
	•	establish behavioral expectations;
	•	reduce fraud and misuse;
	•	encourage respectful interactions;
	•	protect Users and the broader community.
All Users are expected to exercise good judgment, personal responsibility, and reasonable caution while using the Platform.

2. GENERAL COMMUNITY STANDARDS
All Users must:
	•	treat others respectfully and professionally;
	•	communicate honestly and clearly;
	•	comply with applicable laws and regulations;
	•	avoid dangerous, abusive, discriminatory, or threatening conduct;
	•	act responsibly during all interactions.
Users must not:
	•	harass, intimidate, threaten, stalk, or abuse others;
	•	engage in hate speech or discriminatory conduct;
	•	make unwanted romantic, sexual, or inappropriate advances;
	•	use offensive, obscene, or abusive language;
	•	impersonate another person;
	•	misrepresent identity, gender, age, vehicle, or qualifications;
	•	use the Platform for illegal activities.
Savvari maintains zero tolerance for violence, threats, predatory behavior, sexual misconduct, or dangerous conduct.

3. DRIVER SAFETY STANDARDS
Drivers are solely responsible for operating vehicles safely and lawfully.
Drivers must:
	•	possess a valid driving license;
	•	maintain valid registration and insurance where required;
	•	ensure vehicles remain roadworthy and reasonably clean;
	•	comply with all traffic laws and regulations;
	•	drive attentively and responsibly;
	•	ensure seatbelts are available where legally required;
	•	arrive at pickup locations on time where reasonably possible.
Drivers must never:
	•	drive under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or impairing substances;
	•	engage in reckless, dangerous, distracted, or aggressive driving;
	•	exceed legal passenger limits;
	•	permit unauthorized drivers to operate the vehicle;
	•	carry illegal items, weapons, or hazardous materials;
	•	use the Platform for commercial taxi, transport, delivery, or logistics operations.
Drivers are encouraged to:
	•	keep emergency contacts informed;
	•	maintain vehicle maintenance records;
	•	immediately report emergencies or incidents.

4. PASSENGER SAFETY STANDARDS
Passengers must:
	•	arrive at agreed pickup points on time;
	•	communicate delays respectfully;
	•	wear seatbelts where available;
	•	follow reasonable safety instructions;
	•	respect the Driver’s vehicle and personal space;
	•	behave respectfully toward co-passengers.
Passengers must never:
	•	distract the Driver in a dangerous manner;
	•	engage in harassment or threatening conduct;
	•	damage property;
	•	transport illegal substances or prohibited items;
	•	smoke, vape, or consume alcohol without permission;
	•	pressure Drivers into unsafe driving behavior.
Passengers are encouraged to:
	•	verify ride details before entering vehicles;
	•	share trip details with trusted contacts;
	•	use Platform safety features where available.

5. WOMEN-ONLY RIDE EXPECTATIONS
The women-only feature is intended to improve comfort and preference-based ride matching.
Users utilizing women-only features must:
	•	provide truthful identity information;
	•	behave respectfully and responsibly;
	•	avoid misuse of safety-related features.
Misrepresentation of gender identity, fraudulent use of women-only features, or attempts to exploit safety preferences may result in immediate permanent suspension.
Savvari does not guarantee identity accuracy or safety through women-only features.
Users should continue exercising independent judgment and caution.

6. HONESTY AND ACCOUNT INTEGRITY
Users must provide accurate and truthful information at all times.
Users must not:
	•	create fake accounts;
	•	use stolen identities;
	•	falsify documents;
	•	manipulate ratings or reviews;
	•	submit fraudulent reports;
	•	create duplicate accounts after suspension or termination;
	•	share accounts with others.
Savvari may verify information and investigate suspicious behavior.
Fraudulent conduct may result in:
	•	immediate suspension;
	•	permanent termination;
	•	ride cancellation;
	•	legal action;
	•	referral to law enforcement.

7. COMMUNICATION STANDARDS
Users must communicate respectfully through:
	•	in-app messaging;
	•	phone calls;
	•	WhatsApp communications;
	•	other Platform-related channels.
Users must not:
	•	send spam;
	•	send abusive or threatening messages;
	•	repeatedly contact unwilling Users;
	•	solicit unrelated services;
	•	attempt scams or phishing activities.
Savvari may review reports relating to communications for safety, fraud prevention, compliance, and enforcement purposes.

8. PAYMENTS AND OFF-PLATFORM TRANSACTIONS
Users must honor agreed payment obligations.
Users must not:
	•	intentionally avoid Platform fees;
	•	manipulate payments fraudulently;
	•	solicit payments through deceptive methods;
	•	engage in scams or unauthorized financial activity.
Savvari may restrict Users engaging in suspicious payment-related conduct.
Savvari is not responsible for disputes arising from private or off-platform payment arrangements.

9. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
The following activities are strictly prohibited:
	•	commercial transport operations;
	•	illegal activity of any kind;
	•	drug trafficking or illegal substances;
	•	carrying weapons or hazardous materials;
	•	human trafficking;
	•	harassment or abuse;
	•	stalking or intimidation;
	•	identity fraud;
	•	hacking or unauthorized access attempts;
	•	misuse of Platform data;
	•	unauthorized collection of User information;
	•	fake bookings;
	•	rating manipulation;
	•	impersonation;
	•	bypassing suspensions or bans;
	•	dangerous driving behavior;
	•	transporting minors without lawful authority.
Savvari reserves the right to immediately remove Users engaged in prohibited conduct.

10. SAFETY RECOMMENDATIONS
Savvari strongly encourages Users to:
	•	verify vehicle details before entering a Ride;
	•	confirm Driver identity;
	•	share live trip information with trusted contacts;
	•	trust personal instincts regarding safety concerns;
	•	avoid sharing unnecessary sensitive information;
	•	meet in well-lit public locations where possible;
	•	report suspicious behavior immediately.
Users remain solely responsible for their own decisions, actions, and safety precautions.

11. EMERGENCIES AND INCIDENT REPORTING
In emergencies, Users should contact local emergency services or law enforcement immediately.
Users should then notify Savvari through available support channels.
Users are encouraged to report:
	•	unsafe conduct;
	•	harassment;
	•	discrimination;
	•	accidents;
	•	threats;
	•	fraud;
	•	suspicious activity;
	•	policy violations.
Savvari may investigate reports and take action at its discretion.
Users agree to cooperate reasonably with safety investigations.

12. RATINGS AND REVIEWS
Ratings and reviews help maintain community trust.
Users should provide honest and accurate feedback based on genuine experiences.
Users must not:
	•	submit false or retaliatory reviews;
	•	coordinate fake ratings;
	•	manipulate review systems.
Savvari reserves the right to remove ratings or reviews that violate these Guidelines.
Repeated low ratings or credible safety complaints may result in account review, suspension, or termination.

13. PRIVACY AND USER INFORMATION
Users must respect the privacy and confidentiality of others.
Users must not:
	•	publicly disclose another User’s personal information;
	•	collect or store personal information for unrelated purposes;
	•	misuse contact information;
	•	record or distribute conversations, photographs, or videos without consent where prohibited by law.
Unauthorized sharing or misuse of User information may result in permanent account termination.

14. PLATFORM ENFORCEMENT
Savvari may investigate conduct that potentially violates these Guidelines.
Enforcement actions may include:
	•	warnings;
	•	feature restrictions;
	•	temporary suspensions;
	•	ride cancellation;
	•	content removal;
	•	permanent account termination;
	•	referral to law enforcement authorities.
Savvari reserves the right to skip warning stages where conduct involves:
	•	violence;
	•	threats;
	•	fraud;
	•	sexual misconduct;
	•	illegal activity;
	•	severe safety concerns.
Savvari may make enforcement decisions based on:
	•	user reports;
	•	internal reviews;
	•	automated systems;
	•	safety investigations;
	•	legal obligations.
Savvari is not obligated to disclose detailed enforcement methodologies or investigation outcomes.

15. NO GUARANTEE OF SAFETY
While Savvari implements measures intended to improve community safety and trust, no platform can eliminate all risks.
Savvari does not guarantee:
	•	the behavior of Users;
	•	ride safety;
	•	identity accuracy;
	•	compatibility between Users;
	•	crime prevention.
Users participate in all interactions and rides at their own discretion and risk.

16. MODIFICATIONS TO THESE GUIDELINES
Savvari may update these Guidelines at any time.
Updated versions become effective upon posting unless otherwise stated.
Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of revised Guidelines.
